Vision & Mission

Vision

Transforming India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy”, being the vision of Digital India, the IDEA lab can provide opportunities that help the students to “Engage, Explore, Experience, articulate and Excel” in their area of specialization to solidly bolster “Atma Nirbhar Bharat” mission.

Mission

This innovative stimulation intechnical education would usher in qualitative change to the engineering graduates to be more inventive and creative, besides acquiring essential virtues in critical thinking, problem-solving, out of the box thinking, collaboration, communication etc.
